A laptop battery ages with time. This can result in a noticeable change like battery swelling, leaking or just its life lasting shorter. This hinders the portability of your laptop a lot. Carrying a charger everywhere is troublesome. On top of that, a malfunctioning battery adds further risk of explosion. Under such circumstances, it is a wise move to give your laptop for repairing.
